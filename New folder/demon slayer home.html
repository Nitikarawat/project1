DEMON SLAYER

INTRODUCTION

Starting with a TV series that began airing in April of 2019, the story of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no yaiba follows Tanjiro Kamado as he sets off to become a Demon Slayer to turn his sister. Nezuko, back into a human. The series quickly captivated the hearts of fans worldwide with its tragic tale of humans and demons, action-packed sword fighting, and the occasional comic moments between characters. In 2021, the story continues after the TV series "Tanjiro Kamado, Unwavering Resolve Are" for the "Mugen Train Are" as an animated feature film. Tanjiro and his companions head to their next mission aboard the Mugen Train, a task already on track to despair as countless people have gone missing on the train
